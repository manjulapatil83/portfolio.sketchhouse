Residential Building (G+3) - Bangalore, Hubli, Karwar

These designs showcase three modern, multi-story houses with stylish and elegant looks.

Design Features:

  • All houses feature clean lines and a mix of materials like wood, concrete, stone, and glass.
  • Large windows and balconies bring in plenty of natural light and offer outdoor views.
  • Each house has a unique design, with the first house including a terrace with a pergola on the top floor, the second house incorporating both vertical and horizontal lines for added visual interest, and the third house featuring a blend of modern textures and a rooftop terrace.

Exterior Features:

  • The facades combine neutral and earthy tones with natural materials, creating a sophisticated appearance.
  • Greenery, including plants, climbing vines, trees, and shrubs, is integrated into the designs, adding a touch of nature to the modern structures.

These modern house designs blend contemporary architecture with natural elements, creating visually appealing and functional living spaces.

Temple Design

This project showcases a beautifully designed temple, combining traditional architectural elements with modern aesthetics. The 3D rendered image highlights:

  • A traditional gopuram (tower) and ornately crafted wooden entrance.
  • A vibrant color scheme with red, yellow, and earthy tones.
  • Decorative moldings, window grills, and landscaping elements.

Floor Plan:

  • Symmetrical layout with a central sanctum and circumambulatory paths.
  • Clearly defined spaces for rituals and gatherings.

This temple design blends cultural heritage with contemporary design, creating a visually appealing and functional place of worship.
